FAQ: Minecraft MMO Dungeon Crawler

Answers to common questions players search for

Is Mineward a Minecraft MMORPG?

Yes. Mineward blends MMO progression with classic Minecraft dungeon crawler gameplay: parties, builds, raids, and loot.

What version does the server support?

We support Minecraft Java 1.21.5+. Connect using the IP mineward.net.

What makes Mineward one of the best Minecraft MMO servers?

Hand-crafted boss mechanics, gear crafting, and progression-focused dungeons designed for replayability and challenge.

How do I join?

Open Minecraft Java, click Multiplayer, add server mineward.net, then join. You can also visit our Discord for help.

Explore Legendary Dungeons

Each dungeon offers unique challenges, mechanics, and rewards

Tomb of Skarrah

Venture into the Runewoods and face the conjurer Skarrah. Navigate through poison-inflicted minions and mystical forest creatures in this verdant but deadly tomb.

Tomb of Wyreth

Explore the haunted domain of the witherlord Wyreth. Dodge meteors and undead horrors in this dark and foreboding dungeon filled with wither magic.

Tomb of Vorklas

Challenge the mighty Ice Giant Vorklas in his frozen domain. Battle through yetis and avoid the treacherous lava pits hidden beneath the ice.

Tomb of Anvahar

Investigate the mysterious ruins of Anvahar, the lord of time. Discover exotic jewelry and ancient secrets at excavation points throughout this temporal dungeon.

Tomb of Hyrrill

Steal treasures from the underworld itself, but beware - the dragon Hyrrill sleeps fitfully, and one wrong move could awaken this ancient terror.

Ancient Grotto

Delve into the massive cave system beneath Mineward. This sprawling underground realm is filled with bosses, rare amulets, and ancient materials crucial for character advancement.
